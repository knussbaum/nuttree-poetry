Peach Tea Nihilism

It declares, “win NOTHING instantly”
as if instantly, you could grab
nothing, cradle it, caress
its empty form and
marvel at the unsmell,
the untaste.
open this cap and
you might find
Nothing
the tension of opposites
relieved or relived:
a thousand iterations of everything
until all possibilities
sleep and
you remain
with Nothing.
